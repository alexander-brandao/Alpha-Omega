Anatel

The National Telecommunications Agency of Brazil

April 2021 - December 2022

I started my career with technology as an intern at Anatel, at first I developed scripts in the Ruby language for a Ruby On Rails inspection system called "Fiscaliza" but after a year as an intern I was promoted to leader of mobile development where I developed the Mobile application of the agency's inspection system. The application was developed for android tablets, the application was not published in stores because its use was only for Anatel inspectors and the .APK file was installed internally by the company.

The application has features such as showing Brazilian government documents, filling out inspection forms and generating PDF`s from the forms.
The mobile application was developed with Flutter Framework Technology and Dart language.
